Brief description:
Rectangular box chart consisting of 100 brick segments that are colored according to the percentage breakdown of a dataset.

Long description:
Brick Chart gives users the ability to display a percentage breakdown of a category in an alternative format to a donut or pie chart. This visual is useful for showing percentages or positions occupied, such as seats in a legislature. It also easily displays a comparison of each portion of the category in relation to the whole.

Key features include:
•	Tooltip to indicate the category each color represents and its corresponding percentage.
•	Option to include a legend with a customizable title, size, and color.
•	Customizable chart width and height.
